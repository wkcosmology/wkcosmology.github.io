Research Interests

Galaxy-Halo Connection

What is galaxy-halo connection?

Galaxies are born and raised in dark matter halos so we expect their properties tightly correlate with the halo properties. To understand the processes of galaxy formation and evolution, it is crucial to build the connection between the properties of galaxies and dark matter halos.

What am I interested in?
  • How are the galaxy properties correlated with halo properties statistically?
  • What are the physical origins of these connections?
  • How can we test these connections using observational data?

Connecting Galaxies/Structures across Cosmic Time

Why do we want to connect galaxies across cosmic time?

Galaxies at different redshifts are causally irrelevant but statistically connected. High-z galaxies are statistical progenitors of low-z galaxies, and vice versa. By connecting progenitors and descendants, we can directly infer the formation and evolution of galaxies.

How to connect galaxies across cosmic time?

Dark matter halo merger trees describe the formation and evolution of dark matter halos/subhalos as a function of cosmic time. Therefore, once we connect galaxies and dark matter halos at different redshifts, we can also connect galaxies across cosmic time since halos at different redshifts are connected through the merger trees.

Galaxy Quenching

What is galaxy quenching?

Galaxy quenching refers to the rapid fading of star formation activity inside galaxies. There are many physical mechanisms proposed to quench galaxies, which can be broadly divided into two categories: internal processes and external processes. The former comprises the feedback effect from the accretion of central massive black holes, the high angular momentum that suppress gas infalling, the inefficient cooling of gas in massive halos due to the shock heating etc. The later comprises the suppression of gas accretion and the stripping of the circum-galactic medium, the stripping of inter-galactic medium etc.

What am I interested in?
  • How are central galaxies quenched? And what does it relate to the assembly histories of dark matter halos?
  • How are satellite galaxies quenched at different redshifts?
  • How to disentangle internal and external quenching mechanisms?
